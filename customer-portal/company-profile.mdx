---
title: Company Profile
description: View and update your company's name, contact information, and address.
---

The Company Profile page lets you update the business information associated with your customer account. To access it, click the customer menu in the sidebar and select **Company Profile**.

<Note>
You'll only see the Company Profile option if your user account has the edit-profile permission. If you don't see it, contact your provider to request access.
</Note>

## Identity

Your company name as it appears on your account.

## Contact

Your primary business contact details:

- **Email** — The email address associated with your company
- **Phone** — Your business phone number (US and Canadian formats accepted)

## Address

Your company's mailing address, including:

- Street address and apt/suite
- City, state/province, and postal code
- Country (US, Canada, or Australia)

The state/province and postal code labels adjust automatically based on the country you select.
