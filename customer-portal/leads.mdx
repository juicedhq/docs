---
title: Leads
description: View and manage the leads you've purchased.
---

The Leads page shows all leads that have been assigned to you, including any you've returned.

## Lead table

The table displays:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Name** | The lead's name |
| **Lead type** | The type of lead |
| **Address** | The lead's location |
| **Price** | How much you paid for the lead |
| **Received** | When the lead was delivered to you |

Click on any lead to view the full deliverable details.
