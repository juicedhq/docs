title: Company Profile
description: Your business identity, contact info, and support settings.
---

Your company profile contains the core information about your business. This information appears throughout the platform and is shown to your customers in the buyer portal.

To edit your profile, select **Company Profile** from the tenant menu.

## Identity

Your company name as it appears across the platform and to your customers.

## Contact

Primary contact information for your business:

- **Email** — Your main business email
- **Phone** — Your business phone number

## Billing

Your billing address for invoices. This includes:

- Street address and apt/suite
- City, state, and ZIP code
- Country

This is separate from your payment methods, which you manage in [Billing](/features/billing).

## Operating countries

Select the countries where you operate. This setting affects regional features and compliance options available to your account.

## Support

Configure how your customers reach you when they need help:

| Field | Purpose |
|-------|---------|
| **Primary Contact Method** | How customers should contact you (shown in the buyer portal) |
| **Support Email** | The email address customers are directed to for support |
| **Support Portal URL** | Optional link to your helpdesk or support portal |

## 1:1 Default Consent

The **Default Name** field sets the default name used for TCPA compliance when recording consent. This applies to one-to-one consent scenarios where a specific company name is required.
