Juiced is a lead distribution platform. You bring in leads from your sources, classify and price them, and route them to the buyers most willing to pay. We handle the auction mechanics, the wallet math, the deliverability, and the observability so you can focus on the business.

## Who this is for

Juiced is built for operators running a lead-gen business—agencies, aggregators, publishers, and anyone else who buys traffic on one side and sells leads on the other. If you've ever tried to wire up ping-post manually in a spreadsheet, or built a janky cron job to rotate buyers, you're in the right place.

## How the docs are organized

We keep a pretty strict split between "what is this thing" and "how do I do something with it":

- **[Get Started](/get-started/quickstart)** — Onboarding. Start here if you're new.
- **[Features](/features)** — Reference material. What each part of the system is and how it works. Come here to look things up.
- **[Guides](/guides)** — Walkthroughs. Step-by-step instructions to accomplish specific goals.
- **[Customer Portal](/customer-portal)** — Documentation for your lead buyers, not for you. Point your customers here.
- **[API Reference](/api-reference/introduction)** — For developers integrating with Juiced programmatically.
- **[Changelog](/changelog)** — What's new or changed.

If you're brand new, we recommend reading [Core concepts](/get-started/core-concepts) next to pick up the vocabulary, then jumping into the [Quickstart](/get-started/quickstart) to build your first working tenant.
